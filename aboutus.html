About Us

Stop The Spread is a community-based project powered by three volunteers from Greece. It acts as a portal for the public to keep track of the latest news development about COVID-19. The website is an attempt to fight the spread of misinformation in a time where the facts and integrity of what we share with each other are critically important.

Our goal

is to provide context by easily displaying facts and figures published by governments and organizations all around the world along with guidelines and safety measures to protect from COVID-19.
